OVERVIEW

Vardhaman Shikshan Sanstha was established on 15th August 1970 at Kapada Bazar at Malegaon. Initially there were only few students, but as the years have been passed by, the count is reached more than 5000 students till now. Now the shrub of Vardhaman Shikshan Sanstha have been grown into big tree. Accomodating over 5000 students in various mediums consisting Marathi , Semi and English. The campus of Vardhaman Shikshan Sanstha have spread in two halves with great infrastructure having Lift, Library and with well equiped labs for Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Computer.Having a highly qualified and experienced teaching staff makes our students stand out
